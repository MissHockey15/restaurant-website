Lactose Free Options

Our lactose free options use an assortment of ingredients that are do not contain dairy like lactose free milk, non-dairy creamers, rice milk, and soy milk. This allows students to part take in eating many different meals they normally wouldn't be able to eat like pizza, ice cream, yogurt and many other meals.

Gluten Free Options

We offer a variety of meals and ingredients that do not contain gluten in them to accommodate students who may not be able to eat it. Focused a lot on getting gluten free grains so students can eat their favourite meals that could normally contain gluten. Our gluten free food stays separate from our regular meals so there is no cross contamination.

Local Foods

Our staff selects local foods for our kitchen not processed foods found from different places. The fruits and vegetables we used are found right here in Ottawa. Our meats and animal products are from local farms who take care of their animals.
